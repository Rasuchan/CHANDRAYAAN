ABOUT THE EVENT
CHANDRAYAAN UTSAV is an inter collegiate event in honor of Chandrayaan-3, India's third lunar mission following Chandrayaan-1 and Chandrayaan-2 , and its groundbreaking success. Celebrating the exemplary success of the Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO) in landing VIKRAM on the moon's south-pole, which is said to be the most treacherous surface on the moon , this event primarily focuses on novel advances that led to the successful landing of the pragyan rover on the moon's surface
There are three events conducted in the realm of Chandrayaan's success. This event will encompass three sub categories and entrants can participate in either one or all the three events.

SHORTFILM COMPETITION(ONLINE SUBMISSION)
